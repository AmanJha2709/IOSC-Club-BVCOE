Who are We ?

IOSC BVP -Intel oneAPI students club , the first ever Intel collaborated club by the Department of Electronics and Communication engineering of Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering kickstarted it's journey on 7th November, 2022 with its Inaguration and Introductory Workshop. IOSC BVP started with the aim of future of growth in AI, ML, data centric applications and various domains of IOT. The industry needs a programming model that allows developers to take advantage of rapid innovation and creation & IOSC is here to bridge the gap between Industry needs and Students learnings. Iosc bvp a non profit club of bharati vidyapeeth college of engineering that which aims to enhance students skills in technical and non technical fields. A new era of hetrogeneous computing which will accomplished by Conducting cross country Hackathons, Workshops, Project work, Informative sessions etc. As we believe in the path innovation and creation as "Technology is best when brings people together"

Our Mission ?

The Vision of IOSC-BVP club is to help the college students to keep pace with fast changing industry needs and expectations. The club will equip the students with industry relevant skills and build a strong reputation in the industry. This in turn will make the students more employable in the future.
